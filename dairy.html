Global Interdependence and Dairy

The dairy industry in the United States is worth an estimated $45.5 Billion in 2022. The United States is one of the top exporting countries of dairy, only New Zealand and Germany are ahead. The dairy industry includes milk, baby formula, yogurt, etc.

In terms of Continents Europe is the largest milk exporter, they contribute over half of worldwide milk exports. The United States doesn’t have a huge presence in the export of milk, in fact North American only makes up 9% of worldwide exports.

China is a major playing in the global dairy industry; however, they act as an importer. Nearly half of dairy consumed in China is from an outside country.

(World’s Top Exports, Workman)
